# 🌙 YorunoBrowser

> **The Browser for Night Owls.**
>
> Built for one-handed browsing, dark rooms, and people who save too much media.

YorunoBrowser is an iOS browser and media manager designed around a simple idea:

**Your thumb should reach everything.**

Most browsers are designed around websites.

YorunoBrowser is designed around how people actually use their phones at 2AM.

## Why YorunoBrowser?

Modern phones are huge.

Yet most browsers still place important controls at the top of the screen.

YorunoBrowser moves the experience where your thumb already is.

### 🖐 One-Handed First

* Bottom-focused navigation
* Thumb-friendly gestures
* Reachable controls
* Optional site-specific padding

No finger gymnastics.

No phone-dropping acrobatics.
![One-Handed Navigation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/C0A2518617/YorunoBrowser_Official/main/Assets/OneHandedNavigation.png)

---

### 🌑 Made for Dark Rooms

Late-night browsing should not feel like staring into a flashlight.

Features include:

* Forced Dark Mode
* Darker Overlay
* Super Darker OLED Mode
* Reduced eye strain during night use

---

### 📥 Save What Matters

When supported by the website:

* Save videos
* Save audio
* Save images
* Manage downloads locally

Your files stay on your device.

---

### 📁 Browser + File Manager

YorunoBrowser is not just a browser.

It also includes:

* File explorer
* Media viewer
* Grid browsing
* Sorting and filtering
* Conversion tools
* Local media organization

---

### 📖 Comic Reader

Some websites are slow.

Some are full of ads.

Some are both.

Comic Reader provides a cleaner reading experience with lower power consumption and faster navigation.

---

## Who Is This For?

YorunoBrowser is for people who:

* Browse mostly with one hand
* Use large phones
* Stay up late
* Save lots of media
* Want more control than Safari offers

---

## Who Is This NOT For?

YorunoBrowser is probably not for you if:

* Safari already does everything you need
* You only browse a few websites
* You want App Store installation
