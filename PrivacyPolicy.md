# Privacy Policy for Yoruno Browser

Last updated: May 27, 2026

We are the Developer of Yoruno Browser ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any limited information processed through our mobile application, Yoruno Browser (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We DO NOT Collect
Yoruno Browser is designed with a privacy-first approach. We **DO NOT** collect, store, or monitor any of the following personal data:
- Your browsing history, search queries, or bookmarked sites.
- Your downloaded media, files, or content.
- Your passwords, credentials, or sensitive personal information.

All your browsing activities and downloaded files are processed and stored strictly **locally on your own device**, and we have no access to them.

## 2. Information We Collect and Process
To provide our subscription services, we only process the minimum necessary data:
- **Subscription & Authentication Data:** If you subscribe to our paid plans via Patreon, we verify your subscription status using Patreon's secure authentication mechanism to unlock paid features (e.g., unlimited downloads, file conversion).
- **Anonymized Technical Data:** We may process anonymous crash logs or performance data solely to fix bugs and improve the App's stability.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We utilize third-party services to handle payments and subscriptions. These third parties handle your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:
- **Patreon:** Used for monthly subscriptions and billing management. We do not store your financial or credit card information; all transactions are handled securely by Patreon.

## 4. Data Security
We implement reasonable technical measures to ensure that communication between the App and our secure verification mechanisms (such as Patreon authentication) is encrypted via HTTPS. 

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
